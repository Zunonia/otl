Adding a course ^


1. Click one of the courses on the list on the left.
2. If the course is successfully added, following message will appear: "Course added"

Deleting a course ^


1. Click the course that you want to delete from the timetable.

2. To delete course, click OK button at the popup window.

Finding a course ^

Searching by department


Choose a desired department you wish to search at the top left "Department" drop-down menu.

Searching by course type


Choose a desired course you wish to search at the top left "Course Type" drop-down menu.

Searching by course time


To search by desired time, drag the mouse on the timetable.

Searching by course name


Input ired keyword to the top left box, and press enter or click "Apply".

By checking "Search in categories" box and select department and course type, you can search within those categories.

Using multiple timetables ^


To use multiple timetables, click the tabs on the top of timetable.

Resetting/Printing a timetable ^


To reset or print timetable, click the text on the timetable located on the right-hand side.

Checking classroom location ^


1. Locate your mouse cursor on one of the courses on the list, or on the timetable.

2. You can check classroom location in the campus map which is located on the right top side.

Checking past timetables ^


To check your past timetables, use drop-down menu on the top left side of timetable.

Downloading syllabus ^


To open course syllabus, click the icon located on the left-hand side of course name on the course information table.

Checking competition rate ^


The competition rate of a course can be found on the course information table.
Clicking the icon will refresh the competition rate.
※The course capacity given by CAIS is used to calculate competition rates.
※The number of the people who added certain course in at least on of the three timetables is treated as numerator.
